The Technology


We offer 4th generation ceramic membrane micro-filtration solutions for non-biological waste water treatment, which are durable, faster and efficient than existing polymer membrane or conventional filtration process.

Ceramic membrane are prepared by extrusion technique followed by sintering (firing) at high temperature (1000oC to 1500oC). It requires high energy to form (bond), following which below are the advantages of ceramic membrane over polymer membrane:

"Need Innovation (NI)", our partner developed a proprietary sustainable technology for manufacturing ultra filtration (UF) Inorganic (ceramic) membrane and is engaged in manufacturing this UF inorganic membrane and the module/filter equipment/unit for filtration solution under the trade mark of ‘KerasievTM’ in class 9 and 11 for the first time in India. The KerasievTM UF inorganic membrane are capable for handling corrosive and non-corrosive liquids between pH 2 and 13. The membrane has huge life of 15 years compared to 6 -12 months of polymer membrane.

Benifits

  • Env Friendly

  • Durable

  • Low Fouling

Applications

A) Wastewater

a) Industrial waste water: It is PCB mandatory to discharge process wastewater under 20 mg/l COD. And small industries in and around town, city do not have needed land to have WTP. Ceramic membrane based WTP need 80% less space and is the answer. Target industries: Food processing industries to Pharma, Dairy, electrolyses industries, SME and MSME, STP &WTP OEM

b) Domestic wastewater: Target industries/customer: Real estate/promoter: flat, community etc can treat and re-use for gardening, toilet flush etc

B) Drinking water - Surface/ground water treatment

C) Sugar: Primary clarification. Target industries/customer: Sugar mills and OEM’s

D) Distillery. Fermentation broth clarification. Target industries/customer: Distilleries, UB group, etc. Existing OEM working with Distilleries.

E) Pharma: Fermentation broth clarification, yeast clarification etc. Target industries/customer: Pharma industries, Industries existing OEM’s

F) Food and juice Industries: Target industries/customer: Food and juice processing Industries

G) Tea & herbal product: Tea and herbal extract. Target industries/customer: Tea plant, Herbal industries

H) Protein hydrolysate: Protein hydrolysate separation from non-hydrolysate. Target industries/customer: Protein powder process industries, Organic fertilizer processer, animal fodder processor

I) Chloro-Alkali industries: Brine clarification before electrolysis. 

J) Metal recovery: Gold recovery from process wastewater. 

K) Paint industries: Paint recovery. Target industries/customer: Paint industries

L) Steel plant: Hot and cold rolling oil-water treatment. Target industries/customer: Steel plants

M) Petroleum: Heavy oil recovery, furnace oil filtration etc

N) Battery: Acid filtration.

O) Ink (printer): Carbon, ink extraction.

P) Ceramic membrane: Membrane for liquid filtration. Target industries/customer: Filtration, extration and separation process industries
