Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)

Our advanced sewage treatment plants are fully bio-logical, powered by the latest SSB technology, which is the advanced version of SBR technolgy, designed and fabricated in Germany by AQUATO and ATB who are industry leaders and our long time partners.

how to make a site

System Advantages

  • Eco-Friendly cleaning
    fully bio-logical, no chemical involment in the process.
  • No odour
    system is fully aerobic, thus no bad gases will be generated.
  • Render minimum sludge
    typically "sludgeless", but we say with "minimum sludge removal" just for non bio-degradble particles.
  • Quick installation                                                                       plug-n-play system requires only minimum installation time.
  • Safe and easy maintenance                                 
    our unique air-lift technology eliminates the requirement of having moving/ mechanical parts, electricity or submersible pumps underneath the water.  
  • PLC controlled
    driven by a computer system with minimum human interaction. Highly controllable to suite any requirement comes in handy when saving power consumption.
  • Small Footprint                                                                         very compact plant. there's no requirement of a pre-treatment tanks as process is time-based, thus the space requirement is comparatively minimum.

small treatment plants made specially for households

Household Set-up

Upon gravity level, tanks can be designed either under ground or above ground.  

Direct black and grey water to the sewer tank upon necessity of an oil and grease interceptor.

Discharge water can be directly use for gardening or irrigation purposes. Or with a futher disinfection for car washing and toilet- flushing. 

Computer driven, No labour,  extra-cost or maintainance required.
