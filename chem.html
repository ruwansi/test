AquaChem - Chemical Solutions

Batch and Continuous treatment mechanisms are designed to meet the Regulatory discharged standards of authorities, and client preferences.

Incenerators

We offer solutions for managing of non-biodegradable and potentially hazardous solid wastes, in the form of robust, high-temperature incinerators which produce low particulate emissions. We partner with “Inciner8” a globally respected manufacturing organization in UK, offering a range of Incinerators for all applications in the waste management industry. Our products are specifically designed with clean air incineration they are ideal for Medical, Animal, General Municipal waste streams.
